About Hanna

Hanna has over 10 years experience in the fitness field. Her interest in fitness started as a child-- being fit was a way of life in her family growing up. As an adult she stays fit and active with her husband and two teenage sons. Fitness isn't only her job, it's the way she lives her life. Recently Hanna ran the Pikes Peak Marathon with her husband. She's involved in community fitness events including the Prairie Fire Marathon and studio fitness fundraisers. Although strength training is Hanna's person passion she enjoys running and biking.
Owner of Zeal Studio
